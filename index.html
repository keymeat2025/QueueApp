Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work?

You pay a fixed base fee monthly, plus a small per-customer charge. We count customers at the end of each month and bill you accordingly. Simple and transparent!

What counts as a "customer"?

Each person who joins your queue counts as one customer, regardless of party size. So a family of 4 joining together counts as 1 customer, not 4.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect from the next billing cycle. No penalties or hidden fees.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees! We help you get started for free, including QR code generation, display setup, and staff training materials.

What if I have multiple locations?

Each location needs its own plan. Growth and Pro plans support multiple locations at a discount. Contact us for multi-location pricing.
